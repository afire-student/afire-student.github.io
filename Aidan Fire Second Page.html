My Academic Plan for the Spring Semester of 2021


I am majoring in Communication Science and Disorders. This major is more commonly known as speech-language pathology. I chose this major as it is a pre-requisite for audiology, which is my current end goal. I have been interested in audiology for as long as I can remember. My father owns and operates his own private practice in audiology, so I have always had a connection. Unfortunately, UND does not have an audiology program so I will have to attend another college in order to complete that goal.

This semester I am taking 16 credit hours of classes, which are:

  • CHEM 115: Introductory Chemistry
  • CHEM 115L: Introductory Chemistry Lab
  • CSCI 110: Introduction to Computer Science
  • ENGL 130: Composition II: Writing for Public Audiences
  • MUSC 100: Introduction to the Understanding of Music
  • CSD 340: Normal Language Structure
